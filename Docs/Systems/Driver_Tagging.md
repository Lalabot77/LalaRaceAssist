# Driver Tagging

## What it does

Driver Tagging marks known drivers for awareness and presentation categories such as friend, team, or bad-style context.

## First-use setup

Use it to make known drivers easier to recognize on supported surfaces. Keep tags current and treat them as awareness metadata.

## What to trust / review

Trust it as presentation support only. If it feels wrong, review the tagged-driver list/imports and whether the current driver identity matches the saved tag.

## What it does not own

Driver Tagging does not change strategy math, official race data, class resolution, pit logic, or opponent ordering.
