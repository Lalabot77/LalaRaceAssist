# Overlays

Overlays are temporary supporting surfaces for alerts, prompts, comparisons, pit context, message acknowledgement, and focused in-session tasks. They appear when a narrow piece of information needs attention without becoming the main dashboard page.

## When you use them

Use overlays when the system needs to interrupt or supplement the current dash page: warnings, pit prompts, H2H context, messages, or other focused cues.

## What they own

Overlays own temporary presentation and dismissal flow. They can make plugin-owned states more visible at the right moment.

## What they do not own

They do not own health checks, strategy math, pit calculations, traffic/race selection, or persistence. If an overlay feels wrong, review the owning core system rather than the overlay itself.
