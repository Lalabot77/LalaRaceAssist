# Overlays

Overlays are supporting temporary surfaces for alerts, messages, comparisons, pit prompts, and focused in-session tasks. They are presentation surfaces over plugin-owned state.

Overlay content is owned by the relevant core system, such as [Monitor System](../Systems/Monitor_System.md), [Pit System](../Systems/Pit_System.md), [Race Awareness](../Systems/Race_Awareness.md), [Traffic Awareness](../Systems/Traffic_Awareness.md), or [Dashboard Management](../Systems/Dashboard_Management.md).
