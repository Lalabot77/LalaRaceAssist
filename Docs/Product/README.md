# Product Surfaces

Product surfaces are the places a driver or support crew sees and operates Lala Race Assist. They sit above the core systems and technical subsystem docs.

- [Driver Dash](Driver_Dash.md) — tactical in-car support for immediate driving decisions.
- [Strategy Dash](Strategy_Dash.md) — race-management and planning support.
- [Plugin UI](Plugin_UI.md) — setup, profiles, planning, bindings, settings, Standing Start Assist review, and diagnostics.
- [Overlays](Overlays.md) — temporary supporting surfaces for alerts, messages, comparisons, and focused tasks.

Product surfaces own presentation and workflow. Core systems and subsystems own logic, learning, calculations, actions, exports, settings, and persistence.
