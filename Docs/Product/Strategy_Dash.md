# Strategy Dash

Strategy Dash is the strategy, race-management, and planning surface. It supports stint review, fuel guidance, pit-window decisions, race context, and planning workflows for the driver or support crew.

Strategy Dash presents plugin-owned Strategy, Pit System, Race Awareness, Monitor System, and Dashboard Management outputs. It does not own strategy math, fuel learning, pit timing, race-position logic, or persisted settings.

See [Strategy](../Systems/Strategy.md), [Pit System](../Systems/Pit_System.md), and [Strategy System](../Features/Strategy_System.md).
