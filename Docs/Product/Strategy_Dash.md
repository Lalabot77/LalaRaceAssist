# Strategy Dash

Strategy Dash is the race-management and planning surface. It is for stint context, fuel guidance, pit-window decisions, race status, and strategy review while keeping the calculations plugin-owned.

## When you use it

Use it before the race to review the plan, during the race to monitor strategic state, and after changes in conditions or pit timing to confirm the plan still makes sense.

## What it owns

Strategy Dash owns presentation of strategy, pit, race-awareness, and monitor outputs. It may provide dashboard controls for supported plugin actions, but those actions remain plugin-owned.

## What it does not own

It does not own strategy math, fuel learning, pit service timing, race-position logic, settings, profiles, or dashboard contracts.
